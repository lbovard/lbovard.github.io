Scaling Relation¶

For back of the envelope calculations, it is useful to have at hand the scaling relation for neutrino driven wind. This was first calculated by Qian and Woosley 1996 who, using a 1D steady-state Newtonian model, derived an analytic expression (Eq. 58) for the mass loss rate from neutrino driven wind as

\[\dot{M} \propto L_{\nu}^{5/3} \epsilon_{\nu}^{10/3} R^{5/3} M^{-2}.\]

Subsequent improvements by Thompson et al. 2001 using a 1D steady-state general relativistic model suggested similar scaling. Further improvements
