Why do we need neutrinos?¶

It is a natural question to ask why neutrinos are important in merger simulations and what effect do they play in the post-merger.

Neutrino Terminology¶

Here we define some standard terminology that is useful when discussing neutrino interactions.

Neutrino Terminology¶
Symbol Meaning
\(\nu_{e}\) electron neutrino
\(\bar{\nu}_{e}\) electron antineutrino
\(Y_{e}\) electron fraction
\(Y_{p}\) proton fraction
\(Y_{n}\) neutron fraction

Types of reactions¶

There are many different types of reactions that neutrinos can undergo.

The main processes that neutrinos undergo are the following

\[\begin{split}\nu_{e} + n &\rightleftharpoons e^{-} + p,\\ \bar{\nu}_{e} + p &\rightleftharpoons e^{+} + n.\\\end{split}\]
