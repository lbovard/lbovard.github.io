Even Fibonacci numbers

pe link

This simple problem requires look at the even terms. It is straight forward to see that every third term is even. Thus an expression for every third term is desired. A bit of manipulation immediately shows that

$$ F_{n} = 4F_{n-3} + F_{n-6} \Rightarrow a_{n} = 4a_{n-1} + a_{n-2}$$

with the initial condition of $a_{0} = 0, a_{1} = 2$. The solution is now very simple to code.
